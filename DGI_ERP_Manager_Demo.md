# DGI ERP System: Management Overview & Demo

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the DGI ERP System! This Enterprise Resource Planning solution is designed to streamline and optimize all core operations of your gold and jewelry business. From managing inventory and sales to tracking deliveries and customer interactions, our ERP provides a centralized platform for efficient and informed decision-making.

**Our Goal:** To empower DGI Gold with real-time insights, enhanced security, and improved operational efficiency across all departments.

## 2. Key Modules & Business Value

The DGI ERP System is built around several interconnected modules, each designed to address specific business needs:

### 2.1. Dashboard & Analytics
*   **What it is:** A central hub providing a real-time snapshot of your business performance.
*   **Business Value:**
    *   **Instant Insights:** Quickly view today's sales, live gold rates, active orders, total stock value, and cash balance.
    *   **Performance Monitoring:** Track monthly sales trends, order status distribution, and top-performing materials by stock value.
    *   **Proactive Alerts:** Receive immediate notifications for low stock levels, allowing for timely replenishment.
    *   **Drill-down Capability:** Click on any metric to view detailed historical data (e.g., cash balance history, revenue trends, active order details).

### 2.2. Authentication & Security
*   **What it is:** Robust user management and access control mechanisms.
*   **Business Value:**
    *   **Secure Access:** Ensures only authorized personnel can access the system through a secure login process.
    *   **Role-Based Permissions:** Assign different roles (Admin, Manager, Staff) with specific access rights, protecting sensitive data and operations.
    *   **Inactivity Lock Screen:** Automatically locks the screen after 5 minutes of inactivity, requiring password re-entry to prevent unauthorized access if a workstation is left unattended.
    *   **Data Integrity:** Utilizes secure JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for all authenticated interactions, ensuring data sent between the frontend and backend is authentic and untampered.

### 2.3. Product & Inventory Management
*   **What it is:** Comprehensive tools for managing your gold, silver, and other jewelry products.
*   **Business Value:**
    *   **Accurate Stock Control:** Track current stock levels, product details (material, purity, weight, price), and categories.
    *   **Efficient Operations:** Easily add, edit, and delete products, and adjust inventory quantities.
    *   **Reduced Losses:** Minimize overstocking or stockouts with real-time inventory visibility and low stock alerts.

### 2.4. Order & Delivery Management
*   **What it is:** End-to-end management of customer orders from creation to delivery.
*   **Business Value:**
    *   **Streamlined Order Processing:** Create new orders, update statuses, and view detailed order information.
    *   **Transparent Delivery Tracking:** Monitor the status of all deliveries (Delivered, In Transit, Pending, Out for Delivery) and access historical records.
    *   **Improved Customer Satisfaction:** Provide accurate delivery information and ensure timely fulfillment.

### 2.5. Customer Management
*   **What it is:** A centralized database for all customer information.
*   **Business Value:**
    *   **360-Degree Customer View:** Store names, contact details, addresses, GSTIN, and view their complete transaction history.
    *   **Personalized Service:** Access customer data quickly to provide tailored service and support.
    *   **Efficient Record-Keeping:** Maintain organized and easily searchable customer records.

### 2.6. Gold Trading (Buy/Sell)
*   **What it is:** Dedicated modules for recording gold and silver purchase and sale transactions.
*   **Business Value:**
    *   **Accurate Transaction Records:** Document every buy and sell transaction with details like customer, weight, purity, price, and total amount.
    *   **Compliance & Reporting:** Maintain a clear audit trail for all metal trading activities.
    *   **Real-time Pricing:** Leverage live gold rates for accurate pricing during transactions.

### 2.7. Reporting
*   **What it is:** Generate various reports for business analysis.
*   **Business Value:**
    *   **Informed Decision-Making:** Access detailed reports on gold and silver transactions, sales trends, and inventory status.
    *   **Performance Analysis:** Identify trends, assess profitability, and make data-driven strategic decisions.

## 3. Overall Business Benefits

By implementing the DGI ERP System, DGI Gold can expect:

*   **Increased Efficiency:** Automate routine tasks, reduce manual errors, and accelerate operational workflows.
*   **Enhanced Visibility:** Gain a comprehensive, real-time view of all business operations, from sales to inventory.
